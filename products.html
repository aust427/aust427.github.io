Subhalo Particle Visualizer

An application built for visualizing and plotting subhalo particle data from the Illustris and IllustrisTNG simulations. The left panel is an rotatable / zoomable 3D view of the particles, while the right panel is a contour plot over a quantity overlayed a heatmap of a second quantity, calculated from the particles in view.

Libraries:
JavaScript - THREE.js, D3.js, jQuery.js
Python - Flask
