Research and Focuses

I am a member of the astronomy and astrophysics research community, where I conduct galaxy formation and evolution research. I also develop tools for creating, processing, and filtering large volumes of data.

At NASA Goddard, I am involved in Roman Space Telescope (RST) peperations, where I am developing grism and prism simulation pipelines.

On the side, I am using the IllustrisTNG simulation suite for researching the underlying differences between semi-analytic model (SAM) and hydrodynamical simulations created from similar cosmological boxes. This was my primary research focus at the Flatiron Institute.
